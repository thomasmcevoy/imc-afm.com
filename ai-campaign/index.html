CONTEXT PROPOSALS ORGANIZING AI CAMPAIGN CONNECT

Although AFM President Gagliardi has announced an agreement

with the AMPTP which “established critical guardrails against the misuse of AI,” we remain deeply concerned.

Few if any rank and file members have been permitted to see the actual contract terms, and of these some if not all have had to sign "non-disclosure agreements." The Union has refused our requests to share a timetable, and appears ready to keep the contract terms secret until the agreement is mailed out for a ratification vote. Such secrecy deprives members of the opportunity to study and discuss contract language that will impact the future of all recording members.

You can show your support for a democratic AFM by joining us in co-signing our Open Letter to the AFM.* Since ratification ballots were mailed out yesterday, it is no longer possible for the union to share the contract language before mailing, as our Open Letter had urged.
