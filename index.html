The Indie Musicians' Caucus is a coalition of independent artists who want to make the Union effective for all of us.

As indie musicians, we record on independent labels and work in the related network of live venues, festivals, and touring circuits—encompassing all genres of music.

For nearly half a century, the AFM has failed to organize our sector. We believe the reasons for this are structural; we’re advocating the changes needed to make successful organizing possible.
